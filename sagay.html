Sagay Community Tourism Experience

Sagay marine reserve is a 32,000 marine sanctuary and at the center of the coral triangle (center of biodiversity).

Activities that await our guests include a trip to Suyac Island (awarded as green destination) with century old mangroves and snacks prepared by the community.

Lunch at Enting’s featuring Mark, a young Kinilaw Master chef (son of Sagay’s pride, Enting) … yes, see how the best kinilaw in our island is prepared. You may be able to make your own kinilaw from fresh fish to shrimps.

Another community-based project is the bike tour that will take you to Café Albarako by the beach. A refreshing iced coffee awaits everyone before we head back to Silay.

Alternative schedule:

Early Dinner at Penalosa Farm in Victorias.
