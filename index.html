PHILIPPINE CREATIVE TOURISM CONGRESS 2023

Mabuhay! The Department of Tourism in partnership with the Creative Tourism Network, based in Barcelona, Spain is spearheading the development of Creative Tourism in the Philippines. As Creative Tourism is a form of travel that places an emphasis on engaging tourists with “co-creation’ with local culture, arts, and heritage at the forefront, this type of tourism has gained popularity in recent years as more and more travelers seek meaningful and authentic experiences that go beyond traditional tourist attractions. The Philippines with its magnitude of cultural assets, traditions and experiences can definitely take the global stage in Creative Tourism.

Join us as at the Philippine Creative Tourism Congress from December 6-8, 2023 in the magnificent and vibrant province of Negros Occidental and immerse yourself in enlightening plenary sessions and engaging breakout sessions featuring activators from across the country. Together, we will unveil the boundless potential of the Philippines, showcasing its cultural richness from Luzon, Visayas, to Mindanao, positioning them as prime destinations in Asia for Creative Tourism.

Be part of this distinguished event and contribute to the collective narrative shaping the Philippines as a cultural epicenter for genuine and enriching travel encounters.
