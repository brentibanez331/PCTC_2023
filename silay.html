Northern Negros Discovery: Beyond the Sugar Trail

The good life personified in the heritage houses of Negros would often take centerstage when it comes to Talisay and Silay Tours, however, we shall take culture and heritage farther with hands on activity inside he Silay City Art Space and Showroom (Old Silay Puericulture Center). Hand painting of Calatrava bags in the morning.

Travel to Victorias and meet Ramon Penalosa and learn about his advocacy that “no Filipino should go hungry in his homeland”. Lunch.

For those who are not leaving the same day, cap the day with a trip to EB Magalona and enjoy a sunset 7 Hectares degustation.
