7 Hectares: Organic Aquaculture Experience

Take the road less traveled (literally) and take part in a sustainable aquaculture experience at 7 Hectares.

EB Magalona is where Kiko Torno’s fishponds are. Lunch, a gem of a taste workshop, includes a light lecture on the sustainability of a pond’s ecosystem. Learn about the current issues and opportunities for small scale pond owners.

You will also be served a 10-course meal … a degustation that is not like any other.

Afternoon activities will be a running tour of the ancestral homes and a quick lesson on stone art at the Silay City Art Space and Showroom (Old Silay Puericulture Center).

Early dinner at Casa Gamboa before we head to the airport.
